Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

The short version

OmniPrompt's prompt builder runs entirely in your browser. The text you type into the Role, Context, Task, Constraints, and Schema fields is never transmitted to any server — there is no backend, no database, and no account. Your prompts, code, and any data you paste stay on your device.

The only data that leaves your browser is standard, anonymous web analytics and advertising data described below — and only after you accept cookies.

Who we are

This site is operated as an open-source project ("OmniPrompt", "we", "us") and published at https://omnipromptgenerator.xyz. For privacy questions, contact oscargml@gmail.com.

Data stored on your own device

OmniPrompt uses your browser's localStorage to remember a few preferences locally. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Daily streak — the count of consecutive days you compiled a prompt.
  • Cookie choice — whether you accepted or rejected cookies.

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser storage for this site.

Cookies and third-party services

When you accept cookies, we load the following third-party services, which may set cookies and process limited data (such as your IP address and device/browser information) under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — measures aggregate, anonymous usage (page views, which features are used) so we can improve the tool. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense — shows advertising that keeps OmniPrompt free. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve and measure ads. You can manage ad personalization at Google My Ad Center and review how Google uses advertising cookies.
  • Buy Me a Coffee — an optional support/donation widget loaded from buymeacoffee.com, subject to their privacy policy.

Your choices & consent

We use Google Consent Mode. Until you click "Accept" on the cookie banner, analytics and advertising storage are set to denied, and Google services run in a restricted, cookieless mode. You can change your decision at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the site footer.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the GDPR — including access, deletion, and objection. Because we do not collect or store personal data on a server, most requests should be directed to the third-party processors above; for anything else, email us.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

We do not sell your personal information. Advertising cookies may constitute "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising; rejecting cookies via the banner opts you out.

Children

OmniPrompt is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
